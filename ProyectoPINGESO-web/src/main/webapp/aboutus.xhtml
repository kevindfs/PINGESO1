About Us


The “Quality Indices Generator” application is a project carried out by a team of students of Computer Engineering at the “Universidad de Santiago de Chile”. The system aims to facilitate research through the use of indices designed for calculations in bioinformatics, where the user can input a list of genes and get the result of various indices for genetic analysis , through genetic data obtained by GeneOntology, the calculation of indices such as WuPalmer, TBK, among others. The system is open to the entire scientific community
